Hello there,
I'm RACHEL

My name is Rachel Alexander and I’m a UX designer. My path to design is a little different than most. I have a background in Psychology and Business and started to explore careers within the Recruiting field. After a few years, I realized I was ready for something more challenging, exciting, and the change to build powerful and meaningful products.

I enrolled in the UX/UI Bootcamp through the University of Texas, Austin in order to gain the skills I needed to become a productive UX Designer.

I’ve worked on various projects including:

  • responsive design - web, tablet, and mobile
  • website redesign
  • UX Research
  • UI Design
  • Interaction Design


I’m excited to continue to grow my career and expand my skills within UX Design in Austin, Texas. I’m currently open to part-time/freelance opportunities!
